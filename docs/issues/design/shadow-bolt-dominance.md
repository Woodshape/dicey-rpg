# Shadow Bolt Dominance

## Problem

Shadow Bolt (15 dmg, ignores DEF) is the single biggest damage source in the game. It one-shots the Healer (HP=15) and nearly one-shots the Shaman (HP=12). It's the enemy's primary — and often only — win condition.

- Fires ~0.7x/game at Common. When it kills the Healer early, the Warrior loses sustain and the enemy's win rate improves dramatically.
- If it fires late or targets the Warrior (HP=20), it's much less decisive.
- The gap between Shadow Bolt (15) and Heroic Strike (10) is large, and Shadow Bolt ignoring DEF means the Warrior's only defensive stat doesn't help.

## Assessment

**Not necessarily a problem** — big resolve abilities should feel impactful. But the game currently revolves around "did Shadow Bolt hit the Healer early?" as the primary variance driver. This is a symptom of the team asymmetry issue — the enemy has no other path to victory.

## Possible Fixes

- **Reduce Shadow Bolt damage** to 10-12 (still impactful but not a one-shot)
- **Remove "ignores DEF"** — let the Warrior's DEF matter against it
- **Give enemy side alternative win conditions** — if the enemy has sustain or other strong abilities, Shadow Bolt doesn't need to carry alone
- **Leave it** — once team composition is more balanced, Shadow Bolt may feel appropriately powerful rather than dominant
